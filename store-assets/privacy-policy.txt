Firki Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 11, 2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
Firki processes job descriptions and related data to provide boolean search generation services. We collect:
• Job descriptions you analyze
• Usage analytics (anonymized)
• Extension interaction data

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
• Generate boolean search queries
• Extract technical skills from job descriptions
• Improve our AI models
• Provide analytics on usage patterns
• Support and troubleshoot the extension

3. DATA PROCESSING
• Job descriptions are processed by AI models (Gemini & OpenAI)
• Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
• We do not store job descriptions permanently
• Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
We use:
• Google Gemini AI for skill extraction
• OpenAI GPT for boolean generation
• Railway for backend hosting
• Google Analytics for usage tracking

5. DATA RETENTION
• Job descriptions: Processed temporarily, not stored
• Analytics: Retained for 90 days
• User data: Not collected or stored

6. YOUR RIGHTS
• You can disable analytics in extension settings
• You can uninstall the extension at any time
• You can request data deletion (though we don't store personal data)

7. SECURITY
• All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS)
• API keys are stored securely
• No personal information is collected
